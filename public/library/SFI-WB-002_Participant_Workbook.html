INTRODUCTION

How to use this workbook

This workbook does not ask you to explain your life. It asks you to notice what repeats. You do not need to be perfect. You only need to record what appears.

Share only what you choose. The technical interpretation belongs to the operator, not to this workbook.

The 72-hour mark

During three days, do not try to force a decision. Each time one of these thoughts appears, make one mark.

Thought

I want them to look for me.

Thought

I want time from them.

Thought

Maybe tomorrow will change.
